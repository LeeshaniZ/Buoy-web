Welcome to IoT Buoy Monitoring System

This project aims to develop an IoT-based buoy system for lake monitoring, integrating weather and water quality monitoring units. The system collects real-time data and transmits it to two separate ground stations using LoRa communication. Advanced data analysis features help predict climate changes, send alerts for abnormal conditions, and monitor global environmental challenges.

Key Features

Temperature

Real-time temperature data for both water and air, helping to track environmental changes.

pH Level

Monitor water pH levels to ensure a healthy aquatic ecosystem.

Turbidity

Measure water clarity to detect pollution and sediment levels.

Salinity

Track salinity levels in the lake to assess water quality.

TDS

Measure Total Dissolved Solids to evaluate water purity.

Humidity

Track atmospheric humidity to predict weather patterns and climate changes.

Light Intensity

Monitor light intensity to study environmental conditions.

Rain Rate

Measure rainfall to predict weather events and flooding risks.

Wind Speed

Monitor wind speed for weather forecasting.

Wind Direction

Track wind direction to understand weather patterns.

Pressure

Measure atmospheric pressure for weather analysis.

Altitude

Track altitude to understand buoy positioning.

GPS

Monitor the buoy’s location with GPS coordinates.
